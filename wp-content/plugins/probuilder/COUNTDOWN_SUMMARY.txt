🚀 HOW TO USE:

1. CONTENT TAB:
   - Enter target date: 2025-12-31 23:59:59
   - Toggle which units to show
   - Toggle labels on/off
   - Enter expiry message

2. STYLE TAB:
   - Layout Style: Choose Boxes/Inline/Circles
   - Alignment: Left/Center/Right
   - Digit Size: Adjust slider
   - Label Size: Adjust slider
   - Colors: Customize all colors
   - Border Radius: Adjust roundness
   - Separator: For inline layout
