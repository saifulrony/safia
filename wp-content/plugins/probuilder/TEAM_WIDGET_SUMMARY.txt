🚀 HOW TO USE:

1. Drag Team Member widget to your page

2. CONTENT TAB:
   - Upload photo
   - Enter name, position, bio
   - Add email and phone

3. SOCIAL LINKS TAB:
   - Add Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram URLs

4. STYLE TAB:
   - Layout: Choose image position (left/center/right)
   - Content Alignment: Choose text alignment
   - Image Size: Adjust slider
   - Colors: Customize border, name, position colors

5. Done!
