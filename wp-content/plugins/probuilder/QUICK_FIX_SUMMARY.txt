🚀 HOW TO USE:

1. CLEAR YOUR BROWSER CACHE (Important!)
   - Chrome/Firefox: Ctrl + Shift + R
   - Mac: Cmd + Shift + R
   - Or use browser's clear cache option

2. Open ProBuilder Editor

3. Find "Pricing Table" in Content widgets

4. Drag it to your canvas

5. Click "Edit" to customize:
   - Content Tab: Edit text, price, features
   - Style Tab: Customize all colors

6. Works great in a Container with 3 columns!
   Example: Basic | Pro | Enterprise

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

💡 PRO TIP:
Create a 3-column container and add a pricing table to each 
column. Toggle "Featured" on the middle one to highlight your 
recommended plan!
