📦 SHORTCODE WIDGET - HOW TO USE:

1. Drag "Shortcode" widget from Content category
2. Enter your shortcode in the textarea:
   
   Examples:
   [gallery id="123"]
   [contact-form-7 id="456"]
   [woocommerce_cart]
   [elementor-template id="789"]

3. Style it (optional):
   - Background color
   - Padding & margins
   - Border radius
   - Text alignment

4. The shortcode will execute on the frontend!
