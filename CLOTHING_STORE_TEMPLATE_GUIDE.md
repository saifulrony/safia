## Customization Tips

### Colors
The template uses a modern color scheme:
- Primary: `#667eea` (Purple)
- Dark: `#1f2937` (Dark Gray)
- Light: `#f9fafb` (Light Gray)
- Accent: `#db2777` (Pink for women's) and `#2563eb` (Blue for men's)

### Images
Replace Unsplash placeholder images with your own:
- Hero Slider: 1920x800px
- Category Banners: 500x700px
- Product images: Automatically pulled from WooCommerce

### Products
The template queries:
- **Featured Products**: Products marked as "Featured" in WooCommerce
- **New Arrivals**: Most recent products
- Ensure you have products set up in WooCommerce for best results
